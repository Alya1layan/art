About the Artist

Alabi Oluwafolahan David, also known as Fola, is a passionate artist from Port Harcourt, Nigeria. Inspired by the city's vibrant culture, nature, and resilient spirit, Fola uses painting to express emotions and tell stories beyond words. Blending realism with imagination, their art evokes hope, introspection, and connection.
