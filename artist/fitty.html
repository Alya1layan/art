About the Artist

Aderinwale Faruq Adedamola is an afrobeat artist who goes under the stage name of Fitty with The Melody, he is currently a 500 level Mechatronics engineering student in Bells University. He started music since secondary school but didn't take it up as a career. Fitty has found the university has a medium to showcase his hidden talents by showing his freestyles on various platforms in 2022 while in 200 level. His first song Shima was released in 2023 which was a major blow up in Bells University. He is currently the three time artist of the year and a one time entertainer of the year seriously.
